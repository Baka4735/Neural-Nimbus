Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How does the AI-human collaboration work?

Our process begins with AI generating initial concepts and drafts, which our human experts then refine, enhance, and personalize. This combination allows us to deliver high-quality results quickly while maintaining the human touch that makes content truly engaging.

What's the turnaround time for projects?

Turnaround times vary by project type and complexity. Typically, thumbnail designs are delivered within 24-48 hours, blog posts within 3-5 days, and website projects within 2-4 weeks. We can accommodate rush requests for an additional fee.

Can I request revisions?

Absolutely! Each package includes a set number of revisions (typically 2-3 depending on the package). We want you to be completely satisfied with the final product.

How do you ensure content originality?

We use multiple AI plagiarism checkers and our human editors verify all content for originality. All work is guaranteed to be unique and tailored to your brand.

What if I need to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'll complete any work already in progress and you'll retain all rights to the content created during your subscription.
